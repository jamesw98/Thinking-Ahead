Weekday Hours: 7:30am - 8:00pm

General Info: Au Bon Pain is a leading urban bakery café, featuring distinctive bakery items, upscale sandwiches and wraps, salads, and signature soups. There are three locations on campus. The Au Bon Pain locations in Squires Food Court were among the first to open on a college campus in spring 2004. The food court was acquired and renovated by Dining Services in 2003. The Au Bon Pain kiosk, located just outside the café, is open extended hours on weekdays and offers a selection of grab-n-go items. Virginia Tech was awarded Franchise of the Year by the Au Bon Pain Corporation in 2006.
More Info/Peronsal Opinion: I have actually been to this ABP, but personally, it is pretty out of the way compared to other dining halls on campus. That being said, the sandwiches and baked goods are very good if you are in a hurry.
Weekday Hours: 11:00am - 7:00pm
Weekend Hours: 11:00am - 8:00pm
Type of Food Served: Burgers, Fries, Milkshakes
General Info: Burger '37 is an in-house branded contemporary burger joint. The name ties into the year Squires Student Center was built, 1937. Burger '37 features gourmet signature burgers, hand cut fries, and shakes. Burgers include fresh ground beef, turkey, and a house made gluten-free, beans and rice veggie burger. The burgers range from plain and simple to signature made-to-order burgers with a variety of toppings. Guests can even customize their burgers to their own liking. The fries are hand-cut, brined overnight, air dried and fried to create the Burger '37 fry. Vanilla bean ice cream is used to create the diner style milkshakes. They come in several flavors, from chocolate, vanilla, and strawberry to signatures like peanut butter banana, brownie, and salted caramel.
More Info/Personal Opinion: Burger37 is pretty good. I have been there a couple times, but, like the ABP at Squires it is pretty out of the way. Obviously the burgers are good here, but the fries are very hit or miss. The milkshakes are also kind of underwhelming. If you want a good burger, this is one of the better places on campus to go.

Type of Food Served: Chic-Fil-A, Pizza Hut, BBQ, Salads
General Info: Hokie Grill & Co. represents a branding revolution in dining services. It opened in 1991, offering grilled sandwiches, salad and fruit bar, and bakery-style cookies. Then Dining Services added Chick-fil-A and Pizza Hut in 1997, Blue Ridge Barbecue in 2001, and Dunkin' in Fall 2012. Its Chick-fil-A and Pizza Hut are consistently ranked number one in sales on a college campus in the United States. Hokie Grill & Co. is located in Owens Dining Center, which was built in 1940 and named after university mess steward J.J. "Pops" Owens.
More Info/Personal Opinion: HokieGrill is currently my favorite dining hall on campus purely because it has a Chic-Fil-A. If you like Chic-Fil-A, Pizza Hut, or BBQ, this is the place to go.
