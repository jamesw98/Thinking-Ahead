Clubs and Extracurriculars Info

How do I find out what clubs there are?

Joining clubs are a great way to meet new people and find a new hobby, or find people that are into a hobby you already do.

Finding clubs is kind of difficult though, if you don't know what to do. The most popular way to find clubs is Gobblerfest. Gobblerfest is a huge fair at the beginning of each year where most of the clubs around campus will be to give information and sometimes free swag.

Another great way to find clubs is though GobblerConnect. This is a place where you can search for specific clubs or learn about all the other clubs on campus.

What kind of sports can I play?

Playing a sport during your time in college is a great way to stay in shape, and a great way to make friends.

Luckily there are lots of options for sports here at VT. If you have experience in a sport, you can join the Rec Sports team, and if you just want to try something new you can join an intramural team.

That being said, if you don't have experience, you can still come to many Rec Sport practices. For example: the Fencing Club accepts members of all skill level, from beginner to experienced. I can't speak to all Rec Sports, but I know many that do accept beginners

Intermural sports are also a great way to get out of the dorms and have some fun.
